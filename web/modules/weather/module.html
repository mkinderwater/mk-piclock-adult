Weather Panels

Choose the source for each of the three OLED weather panels. INSIDE uses the local AHT10 sensor, OUTSIDE uses current ECCC conditions, Today low / high shows today’s forecast extremes, Hours ahead uses a relative forecast, and Specific time uses the next occurrence of a selected forecast hour.

Panels may repeat a source. Today low / high uses the current local day’s available hourly forecast, includes the current reading, and shows the highest precipitation probability for the day. Hours ahead accepts 1 through 48 hours. Specific time selects the next matching hour in the configured Weather timezone, rolling into tomorrow after that hour has passed.

Weather Warning Chime

Play the built-in message chime when a new active ECCC warning type appears. Repeated refreshes of the same warning do not chime again.

The chime uses the clock's global volume, capped at 55%, and is skipped while music or an alarm is already playing.
